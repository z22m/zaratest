Layered Parfait

  • Cookie Crumble
  • Jelly
  • Whipped Cream
  • Sprinkles
  • Cherries
  1. First you get a cup and put a handful of cookie crumble.
  2. Next you add a layer of whipped cream.
  3. Then you add a few cherries cut in half.
  4. After that you add a spoonful of jelly.
  5. You add another layer of cookie crumble.
  6. Next you add another layer of whipped cream.
  7. Finally you add sprinkles and a cherry on top and serve.

Tropical Juice

  • Strawberry
  • Lemon juice
  • Pineapple
  • Coconut Water
  1. First you pour coconut water into a glass.
  2. Next you add pineapple juice.
  3. After that goes the soaked juice of the strawberries.
  4. Also you add tiny strawberry and pineapple chunks.
  5. Next you add little lemon juice and stir.
  6. Finally you let it set down in the fridge for a few minutes and serve.
